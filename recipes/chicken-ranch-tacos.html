Ranch Chicken Tacos

Description

These ranch chicken tacos are a great change from regular Mexican-style tacos. A quick, cool summer dinner made with leftover rotisserie chicken. Naturally, you can use any type of chicken cooked the way you like; just be sure to shred it for real tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) package flour tortillas
  • 1 (1 pound) package shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 (1 pound) package shredded lettuce
  • 1 (1 pound) package shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar chunky salsa

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place tortillas on a baking sheet, and bake in the preheated oven until warm, about 5 minutes.
  3. Spread ranch dressing mix over the tortillas, and top with salsa, chicken, and cheese. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is ready
  4. Top with lettuce, and serve.
