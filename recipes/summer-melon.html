Summer Watermelon Gazpacho

Description

This twist on classic gazpacho will be a welcome, refreshing sight on those hot summer days!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • Â½ English (seedless) cucumber - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons minced shallot
  • 1/2 cup chopped seeded tomato
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Combine watermelon, tomatoes, bell pepper, cucumber, red onion, shallot and tomato in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour into a large bowl.
  2. Stir in feta cheese and season with salt and pepper. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour before serving.
